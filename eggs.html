Chorizo & Eggs

This Chorizo and Eggs Is Bomb!

Spicy And Rich Chorizo And Eggs Makes The Yummiest Breakfast To Start Your Day!

Ingredients

  • Chorizo
  • 3 Eggs
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook chorizo until ut starts to brown
  2. Add and scramble the eggs in with the chorizo
  3. Add cheese
  4. Cook all three ingredients together until eggs are fully cooked
  5. Serve desired of amount of chorizo and eggs into center of Tortilla and roll your Tortilla
  6. Enjoy!
