Burrito Recipe

This Burrito Is Bomb!

Classic bean and cheese burritos filled with flavorful and creamy refried beans, diced onions, and two types of shredded melty cheese! Ready in less than 30 minutes, these burritos are great for making ahead, meal prep, and even freezing for later.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Beans
  • Cheese
  • Diced Dried Onions
  • Garlic Salt

Steps

  1. Place one can of beans in pan on medium heat and bring bean to a boil.
  2. While waiting for beans to boil add diced dried onions and garlic salt to taste.
  3. Stir to avoid beans burning.
  4. Once beans are boiling add shredded cheese and mix unitl the cheese becomes melted
  5. Remove beans and cheese from heat and let rest for 5 minutes.
  6. Once beans have cooled for 5 minutes, place Tortilla into the micro wave and heat the Tortilla for 15 seconds.
  7. Serve desired of amount of beans into center of Tortilla and roll your Tortilla
  8. Enjoy!
