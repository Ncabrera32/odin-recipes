Mexican Rice

This Rice Is Bomb!

The BEST, truly authentic Mexican rice is so easy to make, and a necessary side dish for all of your favorite Mexican recipes.

Ingredients

  • Long Grain White Rice
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Tomato Bouillon
  • Water

Steps

  1. Toast white rice until golden brown
  2. Add correct amount of water
  3. Add tomato bouillon, onion and garlic powder to taste
  4. Cover the rice in a pan and bring all ingredients to a boil
  5. Reduce heat and let simmer for 20 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
